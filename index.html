ABOUT ME

I am currently working towards Ph.D. degree at University of Florida supervised by Prof. Yuguang "Michael" Fang.

My research interest include security, privacy and wireless networking in a wide range of cyber physical systems such as smart homes, vehicular systems and communication networks.

Before joining University of Florida, I studied at Rutgers University from 2013-2014, and received B.E. (highest Hons.) from School of Electronic Engineering at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2013.
