Wireless Networks, Spring 2017

In the Spring 2017 semester, I was the teaching assistant for EEL6591-Wireless Networks under Prof. Yuguang Fang. I gave lectures on topics of wireless cellular system review (e.g., 2G/3G/4G), radio resource management (e.g., power control, channel allocation) and mobility management. Besides, I helped to update the lecture slides into a more engaging and interactive way, and add some cutting-edge materials, such as 5G cellular system, cognitive radio networks and etc., to them. Here is a slide for radio resource management, which is one of my most interested topics to teach. Hope you could find it interesting as well. :-)
  • Radio resource management​
I also guided students to complete their course projects (e.g., paper survey, ns-2/ns-3 simulation) and held regular office hours to answer students' questions. Of course, I helped the professor in homework and exam grading.

Computer Communication, Fall 2016

In the Fall 2016 semester, I was the teaching assistant for EEL4598/5718-Computer Communication under Prof. Yuguang Fang. I gave lectures on topics of switching and multiplexing, ARQ protocols, HDLC, MAC protocols. I also supervised the course projects, which included socket programming, ns-2/ns-3 simulation, file encryption/transmission/decryption suite design, and etc. The link is as follows if you are interested to learn more.
